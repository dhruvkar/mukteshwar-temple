History & Significance

Where Time Stands Still

Nestled in the village of Langabahal, Odisha, the Shri Shri Mukteshwar Temple has stood for over fifteen centuries as a living abode of Lord Shiva. The name Mukteshwar — "Lord of Liberation" — speaks to the essence of this place: a threshold between the worldly and the boundless.

Unlike the grand temple complexes of Bhubaneswar, Mukteshwar exists in intimate communion with nature. Surrounded by a tranquil lake and ancient trees, the temple sits at the lap of the earth itself — a place where the boundary between stone and spirit dissolves.

For centuries, seekers have been drawn here by an inexplicable pull. The temple's energy is palpable — visitors consistently describe a deep stillness that settles upon them, a quieting of the mind that happens without effort. This is not a place you visit. It is a place that visits you.

Month-Long Immersion

Dwell in the Sacred

A month at Mukteshwar is not a retreat — it is a returning. You will live in the rhythm of the temple, participate in daily worship, and allow the stillness of this ancient place to work upon you. No agenda. No curriculum. Just presence.

🕉

Temple Life

Participate in morning and evening puja. Witness the ancient rituals that have continued unbroken for centuries. The temple opens at dawn — be there.

🧘

Silent Sitting

The temple's energy supports deep meditation naturally. Sit by the lake, beneath the ancient trees, or in the sanctum. Let stillness find you.

🌿

Simple Living

Vegetarian meals. Minimal possessions. Early mornings. The simplicity is not a sacrifice — it is the practice itself. Less becomes infinitely more.

🙏

Seva

Contribute to the temple's upkeep and the community. Cleaning, cooking, gardening — every act becomes an offering when done with awareness.

How to Reach Mukteshwar

Getting There

The temple is located in Mukteswar village, Langabahal, Odisha — far from the usual tourist circuits. This remoteness is part of its power.

  • Nearest Airport: Biju Patnaik International, Bhubaneswar (~300 km)
  • Nearest Railway: Sambalpur Junction (~80 km)
  • By Road: Accessible via NH-55 through Boudh district
  • Local Transport: Auto-rickshaws and shared vehicles from Boudh town
  • PIN Code: 768107
