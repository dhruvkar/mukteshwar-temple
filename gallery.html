About the Collection

Capturing the Sacred

This gallery offers glimpses into the timeless beauty of Mukteshwar Temple and its surroundings. Each image is captured with reverence for the sacred nature of this space, seeking to convey not just the visual beauty but something of the profound stillness that pervades this ancient site.

The collection is continuously growing as visitors and devotees contribute photographs that capture the temple's many moods — from the golden light of dawn puja to the deep silence of midnight meditation, from the lush monsoon greenery to the crisp clarity of winter mornings.

Photography at the temple is welcomed when done with respect and mindfulness. Visitors are encouraged to share their images with the temple community, helping to build this visual testament to the enduring beauty of this sacred space.

📸 Photography Guidelines

  • • Maintain silence during puja and meditation times
  • • Avoid flash photography in the temple interior
  • • Respect other visitors' need for quiet contemplation
  • • Ask permission before photographing people or ceremonies
  • • Share your best images with the temple community
