Location

About the Village

Mukteshwar Temple is located in the village of Langabahal (also known as Mukteswar village) in the Boudh district of Odisha, India. This remote location, far from the tourist circuits, has helped preserve the temple's authentic spiritual atmosphere and ancient traditions.

The village lies in a landscape of rolling hills, dense forests, and pristine lakes — a geography that has remained largely unchanged for centuries. The isolation that once protected the temple from external influences now serves modern seekers who come here to disconnect from worldly distractions and reconnect with deeper truths.

The local community is small and deeply connected to the temple, with many families having served as caretakers and devotees for generations. Visitors will find a warm welcome and genuine hospitality from people for whom spiritual service is a way of life.

Travel Guidance

Preparing for Your Journey

🗺️

Navigation

GPS coordinates: 21.561689°N, 84.366575°E. Download offline maps as mobile connectivity may be limited in remote areas.

🎒

What to Bring

Simple, modest clothing, personal medicine, basic toiletries. The temple provides meals and basic accommodation essentials.

🌦️

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather. Monsoon season (June-September) brings lush beauty but challenging travel conditions.

📞

Communication

Inform someone of your travel plans. Keep emergency contacts handy. The temple can assist with local arrangements if notified in advance.
